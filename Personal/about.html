About

I am a Diploma in Information Technology graduate from the Vaal University of Technology, specializing in Web Development and Business Analysis. I am passionate about building responsive, user-friendly, and modern digital solutions that improve user experience and solve real-world problems.

My technical foundation is supported by several industry-recognized certifications, including IT Essentials, CCNA Routing & Switching, Full Stack Development (IT Varsity), Web Development Fundamentals, and IBM Big Data 101. These certifications have strengthened my abilities in programming, networking, data handling, and system development.

During my time at VUT, I served as an Information Technology Mentor, guiding first-year students academically and personally. This experience developed my leadership, communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills, complementing my technical expertise.

I have worked on multiple hands-on projects, including a Credit Card Fraud Detection System using machine learning, responsive websites, Java applets, and a web-based clinic management system. These projects showcase my ability to design, develop, and deploy practical and impactful solutions.

As a driven and committed IT graduate, I am seeking internship or graduate programme opportunities where I can apply my technical skills, contribute to meaningful projects, and continue developing as an IT professional.
